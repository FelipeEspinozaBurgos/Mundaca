The Historical Background

Dive into the intriguing life of Fermín Mundaca, the legendary pirate whose existence was marked by adventure, unrequited love, and a mysterious death.

Our guided tours and themed shows rekindle this story through storytelling, artifacts, and interactive elements among the original ruins of his once-beloved retreat.
