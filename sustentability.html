Our Sustainability Focus

Hacienda Mundaca is more than a destination; it's a statement on how tourism can actively heal and preserve the ecosystems of Quintana Roo.

Key Sustainable Practices

  • ●

    Solar Power Integration: Utilizing clean energy for all lighting and operational needs, significantly reducing our carbon footprint on the island.

  • ●

    Advanced Water Management: Treated wastewater systems and ecological dry toilets to protect the Caribbean Sea from runoff and drainage issues.

  • ●

    Sargassum Bio-Fertilizer: We transform the local sargassum challenge into a resource, producing organic fertilizer for our native flora conservation.

Environmental Education Initiatives

Education is core to our DNA. Visitors participate in hands-on activities like planting native trees and learning about waste management, ensuring that every guest returns home as an ambassador for Isla Mujeres' environment.

Benefits for the Island

By strictly limiting group sizes and implementing zero-waste policies, we provide a model for high-value, low-impact tourism that benefits the local community while preserving the historical and natural integrity of Hacienda Mundaca.
