Discover the Legend of Fermín Mundaca

The story of Fermín Mundaca a seafarer whose life inspired tales of piracy and romancelives on at Hacienda Mundaca. Settling on Isla Mujeres, he built an impressive estate reflecting his ambition and vision. Today, we honor that history through the "Shadows of Mundaca" historical tour and the captivating nocturnal "Mundaca Show," a narrative performance that brings his intriguing life to life under the stars. Explore the origins of this Caribbean legend and its enduring mystery.

Choose your adventure—day or night—at Hacienda Mundaca Eco-Tour. Both options feature solar-powered paths, environmental education, and immersion in history and nature.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

Hacienda Mundaca Eco-Tour leads heritage ecotourism in the Mexican Caribbean through five green pillars that make us a model for Isla Mujeres.

Solar Energy for Pathways and General Lighting

We use clean solar power to illuminate trails and facilities, reducing environmental impact and ensuring energy autonomy.

Biofertilizer Production from Sargassum

Transforming sargassum into organic fertilizer supports gardens and promotes circular economy solutions for local beaches.

Water Reuse and Treatment System

Capture, treatment, and reuse systems provide sustainable water management in a region facing scarcity.

Dry/Ecological Toilets

Eco-friendly sanitation systems minimize water use and environmental footprint.

Comprehensive Waste Management and Environmental Education Program

Recycling, waste separation, and on-site education foster responsibility among visitors and residents.
