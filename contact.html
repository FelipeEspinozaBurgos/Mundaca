Visit Us

Come explore Hacienda Mundaca in person. Located in the heart of Isla Mujeres, Quintana Roo, our restored eco-historical site awaits with its iconic arch, trails, and stories waiting to be discovered.

  • Address: Hacienda Mundaca, Isla Mujeres, Quintana Roo, Mexico (approx. coordinates: 21.217° N, -86.731° W – middle of the island)
  • How to Get There: Arrive by ferry to Isla Mujeres (from Puerto Juárez or Hotel Zone in Cancún), then take a taxi, rent a golf cart, or bike south along the main road (Carretera a Garrafón). The site is easily accessible from downtown.
  • Hours: Day tours and visits: mornings to late afternoon; Night show: evenings (check current schedule via form or email).
  • Recommendations: Wear comfortable shoes for trails, bring eco-friendly sunscreen and water, and respect the natural surroundings.
